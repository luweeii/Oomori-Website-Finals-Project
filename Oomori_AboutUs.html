ABOUT US

Oomori Japanese Cuisine, your premier destination for authentic Japanese flavors in the heart of the Philippines. Our restaurant has become a cherished spot for both locals and visitors who seek a genuine taste of Japan, especially our renowned sushi, crafted with the finest ingredients and utmost care.

Founded in 2018, Oomori Japanese Cuisine began as a humble family-run eatery with a passion for bringing the rich culinary traditions of Japan to locals in the Philippines. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved dining haven known for our exceptional sushi, delectable ramen, and a variety of other classic Japanese dishes. Our chefs are dedicated to preserving the authenticity of Japanese cuisine while infusing a touch of Filipino warmth and hospitality.

Did You Know?

Did you know that "Oomori" means "large serving" in Japanese? True to our name, we believe in offering generous portions that not only satisfy your hunger but also your soul. Every meal at Oomori is a celebration of flavors, traditions, and the joy of sharing good food with great company.

Come and experience the essence of Japan at Oomori Japanese Cuisine. Whether you are a sushi aficionado or new to Japanese food, we promise a dining experience that will leave you coming back for more.
