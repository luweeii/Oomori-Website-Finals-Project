What is Oomori?

We opened Oomori with one goal in mind: to provide our guests with a unique, delicious, and unforgettable dining experience. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our restaurant. From the moment you step through our doors, you will be immersed in an atmosphere that combines traditional charm with contemporary elegance. Our warm and inviting ambiance is designed to make you feel comfortable and welcomed, setting the stage for an exceptional culinary adventure. We take great pride in our exceptional service, ensuring that every visit to Oomori is a memorable one. Whether you are joining us for a special occasion or a casual meal, we strive to exceed your expectations and create lasting memories
